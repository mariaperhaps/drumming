About

Classically trained as a percussionist, Maria has been playing for more than twenty years. She studied Music Technology at NYU, ultimately focusing her attention on the kit and forming the rock band, Object.

As a private instructor, her teaching style emphasizes technical fundamentals and encourages creativity behind the kit.

Currently Maria is teaching at The Tom Tom Academy in Brooklyn, NY
